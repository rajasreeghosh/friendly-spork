About Us

Background:
The Education and Skill Development Training Division of the Company has kept pace with the dynamics of an ever-changing and evolving canvas of delivering education and skill development and has grown into an Institution focusing on quality, employability and placement support. Some of the important milestones achieved during its journey since 1990 are stated below:

  • Association with Indira Gandhi National Open University.
  • Association with Tata Institute of Social Sciences.
  • National Service Provider manages entire distance education programs at the national level of Sikkim Manipal University, Manipal; Mahrishi Dayanand University, Rohtak and Karnataka State Open University, Mysore.
  • The network of over 600 Learning Centers across India.
  • Life Time Institutional Member of the Indian Society for Technical Education.
  • Project Implementation Agency for implementation of Skill Development Projects sanctioned and funded by the Ministry of Rural Development, Government of India.
  • Execution of Swarn Jayanti Gramin Swarojgar Yojna Skill Development Project for training and placement of 6000 rural youth in the 4 states of Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Rajasthan and Uttar Pradesh.
  • Execution of Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gramin Kaushal Yojna Skill Development Projects for training and placement of over 4000 rural youth in Madhya Pradesh and Gujarat.
  • Exclusive Partner in India of M/S Schuhfried, Austria for sale of its Vienna Test Systems (VTS) and Psychometric Assessment Tools.
